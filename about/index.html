KAUSHIK DAS who is more popularly known by his stage name, DJ ROOP is a Software Engineer, Disc Jockey[DJ], Music Producer, Teacher and a Student. Kaushik comes from a middle class family staying at Kolkata, West Bengal, India, with a dream of doing something different & unique for the society. He has always been fully passionate in whatever tasks he has taken up. The boy started his on-stage performances at the age of 3 as an Indian Classical Singer, then slowly during his high school days his interest shifted towards Electronic Dance Music. Now as a man he has reached hundreds and thousands of hearts through his on-stage appearances as a Disc Jockey and also through his worldwide music releases. DJ ROOP is widely loved for his live performance at public concerts and campus festivals. He sets a perfect example of a multitasking person, by togetherly working into various full-time activities, falling under the industries which are poles apart. His sheer determination towards all his responsibilities and being able to successfully manage them all together, not only surprises his colleagues but also inspires a lot of new young lads.
